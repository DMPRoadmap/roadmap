The Digital Research Alliance of Canada, in partnership with host institution the University of Alberta Library, is pleased to announce the upcoming release of a major update to the DMP Assistant, version 3.1.0.

To complete the update, the DMP Assistant will be unavailable on February 16, 2023 from 08:00 AM-05:00 PM EST.

We apologize for the inconvenience. Please contact support@portagenetwork.ca with any further questions. Thank you!
